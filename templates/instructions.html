How To Use Thrombosense

Expose the adhesive of all electrodes

Open the calf sleeve by removing the velcro from around the edges. Two lines of electrodes should now be visible on the inside. From each electrode, remove the plastic covering so that the adhesive region is exposed.

Place the calf sleeve around your leg

Line up the calf sleeve with the curve of your muscle and apply slight pressure to ensure adhesion of the electrodes to the surface of your leg. Ensure that these electrodes remain oriented perpendicular to each other and then pull the velcro strips around your leg to close the sleeve with a fit that is comfortable for you.

Collect Data on the ThromboSense Website

Navigate to the ThromboSense Website. Register your account and then log in with these credentials to arrive at your dashboard. Begin and end data collection with the buttons on screen at any time while ThromboSense is on your leg. Once you are finished, view your results in terms of a visual graph and a reported bloodflow velocity along with any associated warnings.

FAQ

1Can my doctor see my ThromboSense results?

At the time, ThromboSense is only intended for personal and at-home use. We have not yet expanded into professional healthcare settings. However, ThromboSense is still a powerful tool for your use. Although your doctor cannot make an account linked to yours, feel free to take your results to them!

2 Can the size of my leg impact the accuracy of my results?

No! ThromboSense is designed to be flexible enough to accommodate any size. Our calculations automatically adjust to the diameter of your leg when you register.

3Does ThromboSense work through clothing?

It is preferred that ThromboSense is worn on a bare leg so that data collection can be as accurate as possible.

4 Does ThromboSense require bluetooth to operate?

To use your ThromboSense with its normal wireless function, all that is required is a shared wifi network between your ThromboSense and the device on which you are running the website.

5 What is the ideal data collection time for ThromboSense?

There is none! You may collect data for however long you wish and our website's data display will dynamically adapt to demonstrate this to you! However, we find that only slight fluctuations occur during close time periods so a shorter time may prove more convenient while assuring a similar accuracy!

6 Is ThromboSense a substitute for blood thinning medication?

We would like to emphasize that ThromboSense is a diagnostic tool. ThromboSense is meant to help you identify instances of blood clotting but is by no means a viable substitute for medication to eliminate clots. Always confirm with your healthcare provider before stopping or starting any medication on the basis of your ThromboSense results.

7 What is the lifetime of my ThromboSense?

ThromboSense hardware is designed to last between 4 and 5 years. For those who are frequently active and may be placing additional strain on their ThromboSense, this may shift to 2-3 years.
